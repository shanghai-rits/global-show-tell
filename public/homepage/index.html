NYU Global Show-and-Tell is a festival that celebrates learning and community through a global showcase of student works that emerge within NYU's global network. 

In a world where knowledge once spread through ancient wayfarers, we explore how artificial intelligence mediates global learning and creativity through student works that bring together Artificial Intelligence and interactive media.
