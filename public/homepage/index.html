Global Learning,

& Interactivity

NYU Global Show-and-Tell is a festival that celebrates creativity and community through a global showcase of student works that emerge within NYU's global network.

In a world where knowledge once spread through ancient wayfarers, we explore how artificial intelligence mediates global learning and multidisciplinary collaborations through student works that bring together Artificial Intelligence and interactive media.
